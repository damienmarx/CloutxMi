# CloutxMi Branding Guide

## Brand Identity

CloutxMi represents the pinnacle of sophisticated, alluring online gambling. Our brand embodies luxury, excitement, and professional gaming experience without being overkill.

## Logo

### Primary Logo

The CloutxMi logo features a stylized intertwined 'C' and 'M' enclosed in a gold crescent, symbolizing fortune and opportunity. The design is sleek, modern, and instantly recognizable.

- **File:** `/assets/logos/logo_main.png`
- **Format:** PNG with transparent background
- **Dimensions:** 1024x1024px
- **Color Palette:** Gold (#D4AF37) on dark background

### Logo Usage Guidelines

- **Minimum Size:** 100px width for digital applications
- **Clear Space:** Maintain at least 20px clear space around the logo
- **Background:** Works best on dark backgrounds (charcoal, black, deep navy)
- **Do Not:** Stretch, rotate, or alter the logo proportions

## Color Palette

The CloutxMi color palette combines dark sophistication with gold accents, creating an alluring yet professional aesthetic.

## Voice and Tone

### Brand Voice

CloutxMi's voice is:

- **Sophisticated:** Professional, refined, cultured
- **Exciting:** Energetic, thrilling, engaging
- **Trustworthy:** Honest, transparent, reliable
- **Alluring:** Provocative, enticing, compelling

### Writing Guidelines

- Use active voice and strong verbs
- Keep sentences concise and impactful
- Avoid jargon unless necessary
- Use gambling terminology appropriately
- Maintain a balance between excitement and professionalism

### Example Copy

**Good:** "Experience the thrill of high-stakes gaming with CloutxMi's exclusive table games."

**Avoid:** "Play some games on our website."

## Brand Compliance

### Do's

- ✅ Use the official logo and color palette
- ✅ Maintain professional tone and imagery
- ✅ Use gambling terminology consistently
- ✅ Ensure responsible gambling messaging
- ✅ Verify all claims and odds

### Don'ts

- ❌ Alter or distort the logo
- ❌ Use outdated branding materials
- ❌ Make unsubstantiated claims
- ❌ Use unprofessional or sleazy imagery
- ❌ Ignore responsible gambling guidelines

## Responsible Gambling Messaging

All marketing materials must include responsible gambling messaging:

- "Please gamble responsibly"
- "If you think you have a gambling problem, call 1-800-GAMBLER"
- "Must be 18+ to play"
- "Odds are always in favor of the house"
